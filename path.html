The path

From chat to an operations platform.

Stage 01 · Today

Chat

People use GPT or Claude individually. Company knowledge is limited to uploaded documents; usage has no governance.

The human is the integration layer. Every read and write is manual.

Stage 02

Chat connected to tools

AI reads and writes in your inbox, documents, and tools, but only through the provider's integration list, on the provider's terms.

Every task still takes many rounds of hands-on steering.

Stage 03 · The first step

An agent with context

Context is built around one department. The agent follows a playbook and works directly inside your CRM, ERP, and inbox.

The agent becomes the integration layer. People supervise and approve.

Stage 04 · The destination

A multi-agent platform

Specialised agents share one company knowledge base under a central orchestrator. Your team ships new agents by prompt, no code.

New agents ship in days, not projects. Governance stays central.
